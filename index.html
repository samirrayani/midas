MIDAS

midas is a suite of =Custom_Functions() for accessing artist popularity data from Pandora, Spotify, & Soundcloud directly inside google Sheets

hOW IT WORKS

Google Sheets offers hundreds of built-in functions like AVERAGE, SUM, and VLOOKUP. In addition to these build in functions, Google gives developers access to Google App Scripts that can extend the capabilities of Google Sheets. and write our own custom functions that pull data directly from popular music services like Pandora, Spotify, and Soundcloud.
